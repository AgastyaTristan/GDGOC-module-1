About

The World Tour Summer Festival 2026 brings over 50 local and international artists to 10 major cities around the world, from London to Jakarta.

Each performance features world-class stage production, genres including pop, rock, EDM, and R&B, along with unforgettable live music experiences during the global summer season.

Join our tour and feel the blazing energy of music in an evening full of color and joy, in your favorite city.
